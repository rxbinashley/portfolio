PROJECT WEB

Places app

Time

I worked on this project from October 2019 until January 2020. This was my first individual project from my second year as a Communication and Multimedia Design student. Looking back at the purpose of this project makes me realize how little I knew about UX design back then and how much I've learned in just one year.

Role

Visual design, concept creation

Problem

User statement: "I can't find nice places to have dinner while I'm travelling. When I do find a nice place, I forget to visit there. I don't want to waste my time on looking for the best places to eat, but I still would love to go there."

Outcome

I came up with a concept for an app that helps users find places to eat, hangout or grab a coffee at their travel destination. The problem I wanted to solve was that people want to find nice places to go to without having to look for them so they can save time. While travelling, it's hard to find the best local places in town. Especially when you're a backpacker. The app recommends places as you go, based on your personal interests.
